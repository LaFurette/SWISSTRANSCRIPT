Secure on our servers in Switzerland

Your confidentiality is our absolute priority

Security without compromise

At Swiss Transcript, we understand the importance of your data confidentiality. That's why we have implemented rigorous security measures to protect your sensitive information.

Why is security so important?

Transcriptions often contain sensitive or confidential information: personal data, medical information, business strategies, or private testimonies.

Unlike many transcription services that use American or Chinese cloud infrastructures, Swiss Transcript processes all your data exclusively on secure servers in Switzerland, thus guaranteeing their confidentiality and protection according to the strictest standards.

Our approach to security is not just an additional feature, it's the very foundation of our service.

Our security guarantees

1

Data processing in Switzerland

We use the open-source Mistral language model without using external APIs. All data, particularly sensitive or containing personal and medical information, is securely processed on our servers in Switzerland.

2

End-to-end encryption

We apply end-to-end encryption for all data transfers, enhancing the security of your information. Our processes are transparent and clearly explained, avoiding any "black boxes" in the processing of your data.

3

GDPR compliance

Our practices are in full compliance with the European Union's General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), ensuring legal and secure management of all personal and medical information.

4

Two-factor authentication option

For increased security, we offer the possibility to configure two-factor authentication, strengthening the protection of access to your data.

5

Immediate file deletion

After transcription, source files are immediately deleted from our systems, ensuring that no personal data is kept longer than necessary.

Regulatory compliance

Swiss Transcript complies with the strictest data protection regulations

GDPR

Our service is fully compliant with the European Union's General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), which establishes strict rules regarding the collection, storage, and processing of personal data.

Technological independence

Our infrastructure is completely independent of major American or Chinese cloud providers, allowing us to guarantee that your data is never exposed to less protective foreign jurisdictions.

Frequently asked questions about security

Where is my data stored?
All your data is processed and stored exclusively on our secure servers in Switzerland. We do not use any third-party American or Chinese cloud services for processing or storing your information.
How long do you keep my audio files?
We do not keep your audio files after transcription. Once the transcription process is completed, the source files are immediately and permanently deleted from our systems.
How do you protect my transcriptions?
Your transcriptions are securely stored and are only accessible to you via your password-protected account. We recommend activating two-factor authentication for enhanced security. You can delete your transcriptions at any time.
Do you use my data to train your AI models?
No, we never use your data to train our AI models. Your information is used exclusively for providing the transcription service and is never reused for other purposes.
