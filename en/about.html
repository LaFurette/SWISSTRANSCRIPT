About Swiss Transcript

Founded in 2024 in Lausanne, our company was born out of a very concrete need: to transcribe interviews without sending hours of audio to foreign servers. A simple task… but impossible to achieve without compromising confidentiality.

This is how Swiss Transcript was born; a solution for automatic transcription and summarization, based on local AI and fully hosted in Switzerland. We serve the needs of psychologists, researchers, journalists, public institutions, and companies for whom data security and sovereignty are essential.

The team behind Swiss Transcript
