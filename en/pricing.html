Frequently Asked Questions About Pricing

How does pricing work?

You receive 30 free minutes and up to 3 summaries to test. After that, billing is per minute with two options:

  • Pay-as-you-go: pay per minute, up to 3 downloadable summaries per file.
  • Subscription: 12 CHF/hour or 90 CHF for 10 hours.

Billing is per minute, rounded up to the next minute (e.g. 10:15 → billed as 11 minutes). For larger needs, see our offers or contact us.

Do you offer packages or volume discounts?

Yes, via credit packs (e.g. 10 hours: approx. 25% discount). Customized pricing is also available for businesses (contact us).

How can I purchase a credit pack?

Log in to your account, go to the Buy Credit section. Select the desired pack and pay online—the credit is added to your account immediately.

Does the credit expire?

No, there is no expiration date: you can use it whenever you want.

Can I get an invoice?

Yes. After each transcription, an invoice is available in your account. For recurring needs, we also offer periodic billing (monthly/annual).
