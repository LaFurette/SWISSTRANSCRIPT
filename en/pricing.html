Frequently asked questions about pricing

How is the billed duration calculated?

The billed duration corresponds to the actual duration of your audio file, rounded up to the next minute. For example, a file of 10 minutes and 15 seconds will be billed for 11 minutes.

Can I try the service before paying?

Yes, you can create an account for free and get 5 minutes of free transcription to test our service.

Do the credits in the 10-hour package expire?

No, purchased credits do not expire. You can use them whenever you want.

How does billing work for businesses?

For businesses, we offer customized solutions with monthly or quarterly billing according to your preferences. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.
